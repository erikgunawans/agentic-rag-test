Section 3: Backgrounds, Effects & Shadows

Refine the atmospheric effects to match the zinc palette. Keep the vibe, reduce the noise.

Mesh Background

Current mesh-bg
Purple glow (6%) + Blue glow (30%)
Proposed mesh-bg
Single-hue purple (4% + 3%), heavily blurred

Change: Replace the navy-blue glow with a second purple glow. Both orbs use the same hue as your accent (#8B5CF6) at very low opacity, with added blur(40px) for a softer spread. Monochrome ambient glow reads cleaner on zinc surfaces.
